Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I place an order?
A: You can place an order directly through our website at www.joolca.com or by calling our sales personnel during business hours
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A we accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Afterpay.
Q: Do you ship internationally?
A. Yes, we ship to select countries internationally, Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.
Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Please contact our warranty team for specific return instructions
Q: How can I track my order?
A. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email that you can use to monitor your delivery
Q: Do you have a physical showroom?
A. Yes, you can visit our showroom during business hours at our head office location in Bundoora, Victoria
Q: Where are Joolca products manufactured?
A Joolca products are designed in Australia and manufactured to our specifications in select manufacturing facilities around the world.
Q: How can I become a Joolca dealer?
A: Please email sales@joolca.com with your business details for information about our dealer program.
